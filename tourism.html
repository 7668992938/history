6.TOURISM



Tourist spots in and around Mhow include:

  • Patalpani waterfall
  • Mehndi Kund Water Fall
  • Choral Dam
  • Nakheri Dam
  • Berchha Dam
  • Tinchha Water Fall
  • Janapav hill Temple
  • Jaam Gate
  • Bamniya Kund Water Fall
  • Sitla Mata Water Fall in Manpur
  • Kala Kund
  • Choral River
  • Raj Mahal of Raja Rajendra Singh Solanki
  • Wanchoo Point
